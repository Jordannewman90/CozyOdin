# 🌌 The All-Sleep
**The Meditative God-Sim. Automate the Nine Realms. Take your Divine Nap.**

---

## 🛌 About This Game
You are Odin, and you are tired. Millennia of godhood have left you ready for the **All-Sleep**, but the universe refuses to stay still. 

**The All-Sleep** is a cozy, combat-free automation game where you design self-sustaining runic systems to restore order to the Nine Realms. Spend your day tinkering with magical proxies, then close the game and let the world flourish in real-time. Whether you’re away for an hour or a week, your kingdom is waiting for you.

---

## ✨ THE MEDITATIVE LOOP

### 🤖 DESIGN THE PERFECT FLOW
Construct and program **Runic Proxies**—your divine workforce. Use the intuitive **Alignment Bezel** mini-game to synchronize their mechanical souls. Set them to **Mend** the environment or **Harvest** stardust, and watch as your intricate networks keep entropy at bay.

### 🌈 UNBLOCK A LIVING WORLD
Restoration isn't just about cleaning—it's about **Freedom.** Prune the Chaos Overgrowth and shatter the Primal Ice that chokes the realms. Watch in real-time as your actions allow bewildered citizens, grumpy dwarves, and serene elves to return to their homes. A healthy world is a **Busy World.**

### 🎁 BLESSINGS, NOT TAXES
Toss out the management spreadsheets. In *The All-Sleep*, every resource is yours to keep. But the true joy of godhood lies in **Generosity.** Visit Offering Altars to "Gift" your materials back to the people, triggering instant NPC celebrations and unlocking massive, offline **Festivals** that push back the darkness while you sleep.

### 🤳 THE "JIM" ANOMALY
Amidst the epic Norse architecture wanders **Jim**, an accidental interdimensional tourist from Austin, Texas. He provides zero quests and zero rewards, but he’s always looking for a Wi-Fi signal or a decent taco. Keep an eye on him—he has a knack for getting trapped in the weeds.

---

## 🌿 THE COZY PROMISE
*   **No Combat, No Stress:** The game respects your pace. There are no "Game Overs" or permanent losses—only calls to presence.
*   **Persistent Progress:** The realms simulate in the background using your local clock. Wake up to gift chests, new growth, and a world that missed you.
*   **High-Vibe Aesthetics:** Immerse yourself in a lush, iridescent 16-bit soundscape where every action (shattering ice, planting seeds) feels tactile and satisfying.
