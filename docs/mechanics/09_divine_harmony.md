# Divine Harmony (Domestic Mechanics)

**Category:** Mechanics — Relationship & Hub
**Status:** 📋 Design Phase

---

## 1. Overview

**Divine Harmony** represents the relationship balance between Odin and Frigg. Unlike traditional social systems, it is not about "dating" multiple NPCs. It is about a long-term, domestic partnership that rewards the player for observation and care.

### The "Happy Wife, Happy Life" Philosophy
Odin’s stamina and magical intensity are tied to his peace of mind. A happy home provides the "Ultimate Rest," allowing Odin to automate more effectively and recover faster.

---

## 2. Odin's Intuition (The Discovery Layer)

This mechanic replaces the traditional "Quest Log" for hub activities.

### The "Sigh" System
- **Trigger:** When walking past Frigg or specific palace objects, an icon (a stylized "Sigh" or "Rune of Need") appears briefly.
- **Action:** Interacting with the trigger reveals Odin's **Internal Monologue**.
- **Example:** Odin looks at a broken vase. *Monologue: "Frigg loved that vase. Loki probably 'tripped' over it. I should find some Star-Iron solder in Nidavellir."*

---

## 3. The Harmony Gauge (Hidden Metric)

Harmony is not a progress bar in the HUD. It is represented by the **Ambient State of Asgard.**

| Harmony Level | Visual State | Mechanical Buff |
|:--------------|:-------------|:----------------|
| **Vibrant** | Palace glows with rainbow light; flowers bloom. | +20% Stamina recovery; +10% Proxy Efficiency. |
| **Stable** | Standard iridescent lighting. | Base recovery rates. |
| **Dim** | Palace colors fade to gray; ambient music slows. | -10% Stamina recovery. |
| **Doghouse** | Static gray lighting; Frigg is absent from her usual spot. | -20% Stamina recovery; Odin moves 5% slower. |

---

## 4. The "Husband Favors" (Tiered Tasks)

These tasks are non-trivial and require multi-realm logistics.

### Tier 1: Small Gestures (Early Game)
- **The Rug Fix:** Clearing "Divine Soot" from the floor using a specialized Elven brush.
- **The Raven Feed:** Finding rare berries in Alfheim for Hugin and Munin.

### Tier 2: Structural Improvements (After Phase 1)
- **The Forge Washer:** Fixing the palace stove with a part from Brokk.
- **Tapestry Mending:** Bringing Sun-Silk to Frigg so she can craft new banners.

### Tier 3: Environmental Miracles (After Phase 2)
- **The Balcony Window:** Shattering a palace wall to give Frigg a view of the Yggdrasil sunset.
- **The Eternal Bloom:** Planting a garden on the balcony that never wilts (requires zero chaos in Alfheim for 3 days).

---

## 5. Rewards & Upgrades

- **The Royal Nap:** High Harmony allows Odin to sleep in the Royal Bed, granting a "Well-Rested" buff for the next session.
- **Lunchboxes:** If Harmony is high, Frigg will occasionally give Odin a "Divine Ambrosia" item that instantly restores 50% stamina when used in a realm.
- **Palace Tiers:** Harmony milestones trigger the physical expansion of the Asgardian Temple.
