# 💡 Ideas & Brainstorming Hub

## 🏗️ Refined Alignment Mechanics (The "Cozy Scaling" Suite)

### 1. The "Re-Tuning" UX
*   **Concept**: Instead of discarding or living with a poorly programmed Proxy, the player can bring it back to the **Asgard Proxy Bench**.
*   **Cost**: No materials required—just **Odin's Stamina**.
*   **Gameplay**: This allows players to iterate on their "Perfect System" without resource-anxiety. It turns optimization into a meditative loop.

### 2. The "Perfect Sync" Jackpot
*   **Concept**: Achieving a 100% alignment on all three Bezel rings (a "Divine Sync") grants a special passive bonus to that specific Proxy.
*   **Bonus Ideas**:
    *   **Rare Material Chance**: 5% chance to find "Cinder-Seeds" or "Aura-Quartz."
    *   **Chaos Mending**: A Divine Sync Proxy actively reduces chaos in its radius while working.
    *   **Energy Efficiency**: Proxy operates for 2x longer before needing a Runic Charge.

### 3. Visual & Auditory "Tells" (Readability)
*   **Concept**: You should be able to "hear and see" the quality of your automation just by walking through a realm.
*   **Divine Sync (100%)**: Constant iridescent pulse + choral humming audio.
*   **Stable (70-90%)**: Occasional gold sparks + rhythmic mechanical clicking.
*   **Clumsy (10-30%)**: Flickering light + heavy metallic "clunks" or tripping animations.

### 4. Dynamic Bezel Difficulty
*   **Concept**: As the player unlocks "Advanced Runes" (e.g., *Multi-Realm Logistics* or *Precision Forging*), the mini-game becomes more challenging.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   Faster rotation speeds for outer/inner rings.
    *   Narrower "Perfect" target windows.
    *   Higher stakes for the "Re-Spin" stamina cost.

## 🏰 World & Vibe Improvements

### 5. Asgard Customization (The Creative Outlet)
*   **Concept**: Give the player a reason to spend their "Vault" resources on themselves.
*   **Ideas**:
    *   **Throne Room Decorations**: Changing the tapestries or floor patterns.
    *   **Trophy Display**: Placing rare items found by Divine Sync proxies on pedestals.
    *   **Vibe Coders**: Unlockable NPCs that change the background music or color-grading of the hub.

### 6. The "Delightful" Wake-Up Report
*   **Concept**: Shift the Raven Dashboard from a "Damage Report" to a "Discovery Feed."
*   **Ideas**:
    *   *"Hugin found a strange seed while you were away."*
    *   *"A family of mountain goats has moved into Nidavellir because it was so peaceful."*
    *   *"Unit 4 spent 3 hours staring at a waterfall (it has gained 5% efficiency from the rest)."*

### 7. Emotional Anchors (Relationship System)
*   **Concept**: Move beyond transactional NPCs.
*   **Ideas**:
    *   **Heimdall's Weariness**: He slowly reveals he's as tired as Odin; you can gift him items to "brighten his post."
    *   **Dagr's Letters**: An Elven NPC who sends you handwritten notes or "pressed flowers" while you sleep.
    *   **Glitched Golem**: One specific Proxy that develops a personality (non-buggy) and follows Odin around like a pet.

## 🛠️ Technical Polish

### 8. The "Mechanical Feel" (Haptics)
*   **Concept**: The Bezel shouldn't just be a UI; it should feel like a physical machine.
*   **Ideas**:
    *   **Springy Tweens**: Rings should have a slight "bounce" when they lock.
    *   **Particle Friction**: Sparks flying from the center whenever a ring is spinning.
    *   **Screen Blur**: A subtle radial blur that intensifies as the center Bezel spins at its highest speed.
