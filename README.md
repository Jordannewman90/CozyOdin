## 💤 The Pitch
You are **Odin**, the All-Father, and you are *exhausted*. After millennia of managing the Nine Realms, you’ve decided it’s finally time for the **All-Sleep**—the ultimate divine vacation. 

**The All-Sleep** is a cozy automation game where you build self-sustaining runic systems to restore order to the universe. Automate your chores, unblock the world for your citizens, and watch your kingdom flourish in real-time while you’re away.

---

## ✨ Core Pillars

### 🤖 Meditative Automation
Design and program **Runic Proxies**—magical golems that harvest resources and prune Chaos vines. Fine-tune their "Alignment" via the **Runic Bezel** mini-game to create the perfect supply chain.

### 🛌 Real-Time Persistence (The All-Sleep)
The realms continue to turn in real-world time. Wake up to find your proxies have cleared new paths, harvested rare Star-Iron, and triggered **Festivals** in your honor.

### 🌈 Restoration as Freedom
"Farming" is about **Unblocking.** Clear Chaos from ancient pathways to allow bewildered citizens, elves, and dwarves to return to their homes. A healthy world is a **Busy World.**

### 🤳 The Jim Factor
Encounter **Jim**, a millennial "vibe coder" from Austin, Texas, who accidentally worked remote into the wrong dimension. He’s just looking for a Wi-Fi signal and a decent taco.

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open `project.godot` in **Godot 4.4+**.
3. Press **F5** to wake the All-Father.
